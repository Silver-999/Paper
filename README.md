# Paper

A personal collection of artificial intelligence papers, open-access books, study notes, mathematical derivations, terminology guides, and mind maps.

## Structure

```text
Paper/
├── Book/
├── New_Lau/
│   ├── English/
│   ├── Explanations/
│   ├── Math_Black/
│   ├── Math_Colorful/
│   ├── Mindmap/
│   ├── Paper/
│   ├── index.pdf
│   └── print.xlsx
└── Old_Self/
    ├── Math/
    ├── Mindmap/
    └── Paper/
```

## Contents

- **Book**: selected high-quality open-access books.
- **English**: technical terms, translations, and phonetic notes.
- **Explanations**: structured explanations of individual papers.
- **Math_Black**: detailed mathematical derivations in a print-friendly style.
- **Math_Colorful**: color-enhanced mathematical derivations for easier reading.
- **Mindmap**: compact visual summaries of papers.
- **Paper**: original research papers.
- **index.pdf**: paper sources and links.
- **print.xlsx**: printing order and page index, arranged so page counts are multiples of four.
- **Old_Self**: an earlier collection containing papers, mathematical notes, and mind maps.

The `English`, `Explanations`, `Math`, and `index` materials are carefully typeset with LaTeX.

## Suggested Workflow

1. Find a paper in `index.pdf`.
2. Read the original paper.
3. Use `English` and `Explanations` for support.
4. Study the mathematical notes.
5. Review the corresponding mind map.

Original papers and books belong to their respective authors and publishers. The supporting notes in this repository are intended for personal study, education, and non-commercial use.
